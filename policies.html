Delivery

Early delivery shipments will be delivered at 10:00 AM on the requested delivery date. If your purchase is above $49, the delivery is free.
Repeat Delivery is available form Monday to Friday from 6am to 5pm.

Return Policies

If the item is defective, it can be returned within 60 days with a receipt and the original packaging. Defective items may be exchanged for the same product. If you change your mind after the order, please call customer service or email us. Please note that, frozen food are non-refundable. A refund for the returned item will be issued once it has been received. Returns made after 60 days will be issued into a merchandise credit for the paid amount. You can exchange the product for different products in our company.
